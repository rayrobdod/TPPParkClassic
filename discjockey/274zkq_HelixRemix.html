Praise The Helix REMIX

This remix of Praise The Helix was made with the help of fan submissions!

In the spirit of the crowd-sourced "Twitch Plays Pokémon", in April 2014, Church Of The Helix Choir opened it's doors for anyone to sing or play along with their song "Praise The Helix" for a brand new idea: a crowd-sourced remix. What resulted were a handful of submissions that have now been put together by COTHC into this new re-working of the original, which I am very proud to present!

Thank you for being a part of the Choir, and as appreciation for the support you've given, this song is "pay if you want". Download it for free... or leave a donation if you like.

Give a major kudos to the awesome folks who put themselves out there to be a part of this! I hope we can do this again in the future-and make it bigger next time!

If you want to give your support, it goes towards more Church Of The Helix Choir songs and more music from me (Mark) so I can keep doing this in the future! Thank you so much for being a part of the Choir and all the support you've given thus far.

Helix Bless You! Praise the Helix!!!
